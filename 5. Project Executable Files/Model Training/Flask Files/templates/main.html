About the Project

This project is designed to classify five types of rice using a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN). The five types of rice that can be classified are:

  • Arborio
  • Basmati
  • Ipsala
  • Jasmine
  • Karacadag

Project Usefulness

The Rice Classification Project is highly useful in various sectors:

  • Agriculture: Helps farmers and agricultural experts in identifying and categorizing different types of rice, ensuring the right type is grown and harvested.
  • Food Industry: Assists in quality control and sorting processes in rice milling and packaging plants, leading to better product differentiation and market segmentation.
  • Research: Provides a tool for researchers to analyze rice samples, leading to advancements in agricultural research and development.
  • Consumer Awareness: Educates consumers about different types of rice, helping them make informed choices about their food purchases.
