Rice Type Classification

Rice is one of the most important staple crops in the world, providing a significant portion of the world's food supply. Given its widespread cultivation and consumption, image classification of rice crops can be a valuable tool for agricultural management and production. Image classification of rice crops involves the use of computer vision techniques to analyze and categorize digital images of rice fields or plants. This process can provide valuable insights into various aspects of rice cultivation, such as plant health, growth stage, pest and disease detection, and yield estimation.
